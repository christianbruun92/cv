Christian Bruun

Innovation & Design Engineer, MSc.Eng.

I am passionate about creating great user experiences,
and I do so by combining qualitative methods with creativity and systematic engineering.
To me, UX is so much more than what exists on a screen.
I believe that one must consider the complete interface between the user(s) and the product;
the physical, the digital and the courses of action in which the interactions can and will emerge.

Even though I only have two, my profile allows me to have a foot in several camps;
the designers, the engineers, management, and of course those who are going to use the product.
I bridge the gap between the users, and that which is possible within the organisation.
